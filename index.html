IntuitiveRobotics

This website is part of our final project for the Intro to Robotic Manipulation course. It is a visual learning tool designed to help students understand key robotics concepts such as Forward Kinematics, Inverse Kinematics, and Space Jacobians.

We aim to make robotics more intuitive and interactive for learners by combining code, visualization, and theory.
